# Email P60

### P60 Email.

This lets you email P60 to your employees.    
A P60 must be issued to every employee who is employed at the end of the year. Leavers do not get P60s.  
P60 must be issued by 31st May.  

**Range**  
Select From & To if you wish to restrict the employees.

**Return Email**  
Enter your return email address.

**Signed**  
Enter name of sender.
