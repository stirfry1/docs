# Calculate 8 Weeks Pay.

### This is determines what start date should be used to then determine what the average 8 weeks pay was.  

Enter the Date of the Event, ie first Sick day, Expected Week of ChildBirth, Adoption, Death of Child, Neonatal Birth date.  
And press 'Enter' on the date. This will populate the **Eight Weeks Pay** grid.  
These figures can be changed, manually.  

This will inform you if the employee has:  
Not worked long enough.  
The earnings are too low.

CalcPay will calculate the **Average pay**. When you **Save** this figure will be taken back to the calling procedure.
