# Employee Details S.N.C.P.

### STATUTORY NEONATAL CARE PAY

For employees in England, Scotland, and Wales who have a baby requiring a continuous period of at least seven days of neonatal care, within the first 28 days of birth.  
Each continuous seven days allows a weeks SNCP.  
Maximum weeks for SNCP are 12.  
Both the Mother/Adopter and Partner are entitled to it.
The Mother/Adopter will normally take it after their SMP/SAP, it cannot be given at the same time as SMP/SAP.
The Partner can claim their weeks at anytime in the 68 week period.  
If the baby is in neonatal care, or in the first week after, Partners can take it in blocks of at least one week at a time (Tier 1).  
If it has been more than a week since the baby left neonatal care (Tier 2), employees must take any leave they’re entitled to in one single continuous block.  
The employee should complete a NEO3 form or equivalent.  
https://www.tax.service.gov.uk/guidance/claim-statutory-neonatal-care-pay/start/about-this-form

You cannot get SSP and SNCP at the same time.  

All paid leave must be taken within 68 weeks of child birth.  

For PAID LEAVE they need to be employed for at least 26 weeks up to the end of the ‘qualifying week’.  
For LEAVE it is from the first day of employment.  

HMRC calculator may show the calculation on a daily basis, CalcPay calculates on a weekly basis, the week needs to be completed in the pay period.

**Apply S.N.C.P.**  
Tick this to enable the boxes below.

**Calculate Average Pay**  
This brings up the Average of 8 weeks pay form, when you enter the date, the amounts of previous pay should come up, which can be altered.
The average pay is calculated and brought back to the main form.

**Weeks Claimed**  
No of weeks to be claimed.

**Payment Grid**  
You enter the Start Date, and No of weeks to take.  

**Add Period**  
This will allow you to add a record to the grid.  
You can then enter the start Date and the No of weeks to be taken. There can be more than one Start Date for 
Tier1 employees. For Tier2 employees it should be continuous.  

**Finished**  
CalcPay will automatically tick the finished flag.  
You can manually tick it to prevent any more calculations.

**RTI/EPS**  
This must be sent in for each month you are claiming SNCP.

**Records re SNCP,**  
The payment details of the above are recorded on the P11. You should keep a note of weeks not paid.  
CalcPay will automatically calculate the recovery of SNCP in the P30 monthly payments, please ensure that, if you are a 'Small Employer', you have ticked  'Allowed small employers relief' in 'Employer/Employer Details'.
